Event Eliminations Results

December 5th, 2020


Results are available here.


Thank you for your hard work these past few weeks! We are excited to announce the event eliminations results and hope that your preparation for the upcoming state check-in occurring on January 8th, 2021 goes smoothly.


Grading for each event was led by one or two officers not registered for the event, followed by a final confirmation conducted by the entire team (again, only by those not registered for the event). Note that officers were randomly selected to share the folders for event submission.


From this moment onwards, any teams that have advanced through event eliminations will be locked in. Teams may continue to add or remove members as they please, but no new teams may join these events unless a team currently locked in decides to drop the event. If so, this opening will become available on a first-come-first-serve basis. We will have all rubrics available for all teams after we get back from break, but you may also request a scanned copy (over Remind, please include the event name and team number) to be dropped in your folder for use over winter break.

Club Fair Meeting

September 11th, 2020, 11:00-11:15 am


Today's meeting was a way for us to be able introduce what TSA is to anyone interested. Access the presentation here.


Through competitive events, TSA offers a chance for students to create projects and showcase them at a series of fun and engaging conferences. Our chapter here at Inglemoor makes sure that we have our own events that allow students to get engaged in our STEM community, prep for their events, and have fun with club members all across the school.


The first general membership meeting will happen on September 16th, 2020, at 1:00. Come to meet the officer team and learn more about club registration, deadlines, and updates!


Our Regional Conference will be sometime early January or February 2021. We recommend you pick your events soon and get started right away. As a reminder, there will be no LEAP required for Regionals or States.
